## Notes Structure

Each paper's notes follow this template:
- **Summary**: Core contribution in 2-3 sentences
- **Key Concepts**: Main theoretical concepts introduced

- **Key Results**: Empirical findings and benchmarks
- **Relevance to Project**: Direct connections to our research
- **Questions/Ideas**: Open questions and research directions
- **Quotes**: Important verbatim excerpts
- **Related Papers**: Connected work to explore

- **Next Steps**: Action items from this paper
